Teaching and mentorship are central to who I am as a scientist. I believe that 
learning happens best in inclusive, supportive environments where students feel 
seen and challenged at the same time. My approach draws on active learning, 
reflection, and open dialogue — whether I am leading a seminar, guiding a lab 
session, or sitting down with a student navigating a difficult moment in their 
academic journey.
