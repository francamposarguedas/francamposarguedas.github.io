My research focuses on how woody plants respond to temperature, with a 
particular interest in cold hardiness, dormancy, and the consequences of 
climate warming for freeze risk. I combine controlled-environment experiments, 
field-based observations, and physiological modeling to understand how plants 
prepare for, survive, and recover from cold temperatures.
