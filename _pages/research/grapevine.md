One of the collaborative projects during my Ph.D. examines cold hardiness dynamics in grapevines and how they can be used to predict budbreak timing and associated freeze risks. This work, developed in collaboration with researchers across the US and France, shows how cold hardiness trajectories through the season can be used to anticipate when vines become vulnerable to freezing events, a critical question as spring frost risks shift with climate change.

**Publication**

Campos-Arguedas, F.\*, Kirchhof, E.\*, et al. (2026). Cold hardiness dynamics predict 
budbreak and associated low-temperature threats in grapevine. *New Phytologist.* 
[https://doi.org/10.1111/nph.71074](https://doi.org/10.1111/nph.71074)
